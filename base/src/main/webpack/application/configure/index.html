Register MechJackBot with Twitch

MechJackBot makes use of Twtich Authentication and the Twitch API. Twitch requires that applications that make use of these services are registed and have a unique Client ID. MechJackBot will also require the Client Secret Twitch provides.

How to Register MechJackBot with Twitch

  1. Go to https://dev.twitch.tv/.

  2. If you aren't already logged in to a Twitch developer account, click on Sign up with Twitch in the upper right hand corner of the page and then click on Authorize to sign in to or create your developer account.

  3. Once you are logged in to your Twitch developer account, go to your Dashboard, select the Apps tab and click Register Your Application to register a new application with Twitch.

  4. Choose a name for your application. It doesn't matter what you choose as long as it's unique. I suggest something like <TwitchLogin>Bot, where you replace <TwitchLogin> with your Twitch login.

    App names must not include “Twitch” (as an exact or fuzzy match), or registration will fail.

  5. Tell Twitch where to send authentication requests by setting an OAuth Redirect URL. Since you're running MechJackBot on your local machine use http://localhost:8080/.

    MechJackBot is not ready to be hosted on the public Internet. DON'T DO IT.

  6. Select Chat Bot as your category and click Create.

  7. You'll be brought to your Developer Applications page with your new app listed. Click on Manage for your new app.

  8. Toward the bottom of the page you'll see your app's Client ID. Client IDs are public and can be shared. Copy and paste it here.

  9. Click on New Secret and then OK to generate a new Client Secret. Client secrets are equivalent to a password for your application and must be kept confidential. Copy and paste it here.
